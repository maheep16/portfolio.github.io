Data Anaysis Projet

Group project from STA304 (Surveys, Sampling and Observational Data)

Created: November 2025

Description:Anaylsis on Toronto crime victims from 2014 to 2023. Our anayslis aimed to answer the following 3 questions:
1. Does the average number of reported crimes per category differ significantly across years?
2. Is there an association between sex and the type of crime experienced?
3. Are certain age groups more frequently victims of specific crime types?
